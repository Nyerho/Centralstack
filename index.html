Our Story

Founded in 2018, Central Trade Hub emerged from a vision to democratize access to global financial markets. Our team of experienced traders, financial engineers, and technology experts came together with a mission to create the most advanced, user-friendly trading platform in the industry.

Today, we serve over 180,000 active traders across 45 countries, processing more than $4.2 billion in daily trading volume. Our commitment to innovation, security, and customer success has made us one of the fastest-growing fintech companies in the trading space.
